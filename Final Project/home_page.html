Interesting Facts

      The Yankees don't put their players names on the back of the jerseys because the owners wanted to keep with the old school tradition of the jerseys.
      From 1903 to 1913 the Yankees were not the Yankees, they were the highlanders.
      Since 1973 the Yankees have been owned by the Steinbrenner family.
      The Yankees are the most successful MLB team ever with 27 World Series wins.
      The Yankees' nickname is the Bronx bombers. This nickname came from the media who call homeruns bombs and the Yankees are well known for hitting a lot of homeruns.
      The Yankees have an official theme song called 'Here Comes The Yankees' which gets played at their home games.
      The Yankees have the most retired numbers in the MLB with a total of 22 retired numbers.
